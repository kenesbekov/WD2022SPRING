Recipe #1 - Avocado dip

  • Time: 10 min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
fat-free sour cream 1/2 cup
chopped onion 2 teaspoons
hot sauce 1/8 teaspoon
avocado, peeled, pitted and mashed 1 ripe

Step 1: In a small bowl, combine sour cream, onion, hot sauce and avocado.

Step 2: Mix to blend the ingredients evenly.

Step 3: Serve with baked tortilla chips or sliced vegetables.

Recipe #2 - Cheese latkes

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
part-skim ricotta 1 cup
whole-wheat flour 3/4 cup
eggs 3
sugar 1 tablespoon
double-acting baking powder 1/2 teaspoon
salt 1/2 teaspoon

Step 1: In a large bowl, mix all ingredients together until you have a thick batter.

Step 2: Lightly grease skillet and heat over medium heat. Spoon 1-2 tablespoons of batter onto hot skillet. Spread the batter into a thin circle.

Step 3: Fry each latke for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown. Reduce heat if the outside browns faster than the inside is cooking. Serve warm.

Recipe #3 - Carne asada

  • Time: 4 hours
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
skirt steak 1 pound
low-sodium soy sauce 1/4 cup
olive oil 1/4 cup
limes, juiced 2
minced garlic 2 tablespoons
chopped cilantro 2 tablespoons
Maggi seasoning sauce 1 tablespoon
sugar 1 tablespoon
kosher salt 1/4 tablespoon

Step 1: Clean and trim the excess fat and tissue from steak. The steak may need to be cut down to a size that will lay flat on the grill or cast-iron skillet.

Step 2: In a glass or plastic bowl, combine the soy sauce, oil, lime juice, garlic, cilantro, Maggi seasoning and sugar. Place the clean steak in the marinade and let marinate 4 hours or overnight in the refrigerator.

Step 3: Remove meat from the refrigerator and let sit out at room temperature for 20 minutes. Heat a grill or cast-iron skillet to high. Place the meat on the hot grill or skillet. Cook for 2 to 4 minutes a side. Place the meat on a cutting board and let rest.

Step 4: Cut the steak into 2-inch thick pieces, then turn and cut against the grain. Sprinkle meat with salt and serve.
